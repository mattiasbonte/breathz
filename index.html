For practitioners

Build the exact exercise you teach, send one link. Your client taps it and is breathing in five seconds — no install, no account, no cost, and it keeps working offline.

  1. Shape the practice. Pick a preset or create your own — sliders, or plain text like in 4 / hold 7 / out 8. Set the cycles, choose the animation, and add an intention phrase if you like.
  2. Share. The link carries everything: pattern, parts, name, animation and intention. Add &by=Your%20Name to the end and the practice arrives signed by you. The QR button on any practice makes a printable code for studio walls and handout cards.
  3. They breathe. It opens ready on any phone or laptop. Nothing is tracked and nothing leaves their device — their practice log stays local, and they can copy it to send you if you work that way.
